I am a Marine Corps Veteran who has seen more in my short time in the Marine Corps then I can write about right now. I came back to Cheney after getting out in 2011 with every intention of finding that next chapter in my life. Just so happens it was in Graphic Design. Some of my facorite type of work to do is Logo Design and Poster Design. I like the simple yet effective approach when working on this. I don't like to get too crazy in my work or too technichal either. Just portray what needs to be there yet making it pleasing and effective to the audience. I want my brand to be percieved as a simple yet effective solution to my designs. I get a lot of inspiration from the designer Rand Paul. This guy new how to get things done!
